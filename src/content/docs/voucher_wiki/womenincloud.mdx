## 🔹 Who Can Apply?
✅ Open to all genders, **priority for women and other marginalized groups**.
✅ Must complete a learning path on Coursera.

- **DP-100**: Azure Data Scientist  
- **PL-300**: Power BI Data Analyst  
- **MO-210**: Project Management  
- **MO-300**: UX Design

- **SC-900**: Cybersecurity Fundamentals  
- **DP-900**: Data Fundamentals

- **PL-900**: Power Platform Fundamentals
