You may qualify for a 50% discount on the DP-700 exam if you meet one of the following criteria:

- Passed **DP-203**  
- [Completed the DP-700 Learning Plan](https://learn.microsoft.com/plans/3gjnikype48pd7?WT.mc_id=studentamb_364737)  
- Attended a **Get Certified: DP-700** [live or on-demand learning session](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/shows/learn-live/microsoft-fabric-learn-together/?WT.mc_id=studentamb_364737)

## Eligibility Requirements

To take advantage of this limited-time offer, you must:

1. Be a member of the **Fabric Community** (join if you’re not already).  
2. Have a **Microsoft Learn profile** and provide your Microsoft Learn username.  
3. Not already be a **Microsoft Certified: Fabric Data Engineer Associate (Exam DP-700)**.  
4. Meet one of the following criteria:  
   - Passed the DP-203 exam, OR  
   - Completed all modules in the DP-700 Fabric Learning Plan, OR  
   - Attended a **Get Certified: DP-700** live or on-demand session.  
5. Be confident you can schedule and pass the DP-700 exam within **30 days** of receiving the discount voucher.  
6. Agree to the [terms and conditions](https://aka.ms/IamReady/DP700Terms?ocid=fabric25_iamready_campaignpage_azdata).
